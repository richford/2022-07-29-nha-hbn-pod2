HBN-POD2 Neurohackademy use cases

You might be interested in using HBN-POD2 for the hackathon if any of the following apply:

  • You have an interest in pediatric and adolescent mental health or neuroimaging.
  • You have current or anticipated access to the phenotypical data.
  • You want to examine the open phenotypes (age, sex, handedness, QC) in a large cross-sectional dataset (N=2,136).
  • You want to benchmark your own preprocessing methods development.
  • You are interested in QC of large neuroimaging datasets.
